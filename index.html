My name is Long Vu, I'm a student studying at The Australian National University who currently lives in Canberra. Growing up in a technology-driven generation, my daily experience is evidence that art and tech function hand in hand. I can't help but imagine how a design can be interacted with, beyond simply observing, in a 3D space, on someone's mobile device, or on a physical product. Also, I believe that design something meaningful requires spending time on concepts, and the more meticulous of the interpretation will show the better result of the final presentation. Knowing that the concept of 3D and virtual reality are gradually becoming significant to a graphic designer, I continue studying at The New School to achieve a BFA in Technology and Design
